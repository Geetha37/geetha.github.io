FAQ

1.Can you ship internationally or process internationally credits cards?
At this time we do not offer internationally shipping and cannot accept credits cards from outside of the India.
2. How can I pay for my order?
Clothing Shop Online currently accepts Phone Pay,Google Pay or any credit card within IndiaS.
3. How long will it take me to get my order?
For standard shipping, you should receive your order within 4-5 business days.
4. How can I check the status of my order?
We know you can't wait to receive your order, which is why we will be sure to notify you when your order ships via email.
You can also see the current status of your order here.
5. Can I cancel or change my order?
We begin processing orders as soon as they are placed, for this reason we are unable to cancel or make changes to orders.
Returns can be initiated through our returns page.
6.Can I exchange my order?
We do not offer exchanges at this time.
If you would like to make an exchange, simply return the unused goods back to us
using our returns page and make a new purchase for the item or items you want through our site.
7.How do I return my order?
You should return before 30 days of delivery
