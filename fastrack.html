FASTRACK

Fastrack is a fashion accessory retail brand in India.
The company was launched in 1998 as a sub-brand of Titan Watches.
In 2005, Fastrack was spun off as an independent brand targeting the urban youth and growing fashion industry in India.
Fastrack began opening retail stores throughout the country. The first store opened in early 2009.
